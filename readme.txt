== Description ==
 
Insert custom content between paragraphs, choose after which paragraph you want to insert your custom content.
Now you can insert custom HTML content between your paragraphs using this simple and lightweight plugin.

For Example...

From CBP page, insert your custom content in the "Content Field" after 10 paragraphs.
If your article/page have less than 10 paragraphs your custom content will not be displayed.

Enjoy your new Wordpress Plugin.

== Installation ==
 
1. Upload folder inside zip `content-between-paragraphs.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to CBP settings page on your admin menu
